The Family User Journey: From Discovery to Empowerment

This journey illustrates how the integrated system creates a seamless, supportive, and dignified experience for a family applying for benefits.

1

Discovery & Screening

A parent visits the fast, accessible Informational Website and uses the anonymous Universal Benefits Screener to get an instant eligibility estimate, building trust from the first interaction.

2

Secure Application

They create an account via the Digital Identity service and complete the intuitive Application Form, electronically signing consent forms with the integrated Document Signing service.

3

Automated Verification

Submission triggers automated, parallel checks. The system calls the Income Verification API and notifies the user of progress via the CX Platform (SMS/Email).

4

Approval & Ongoing Management

After a quick review in the Administrative Portal, the family is approved. They can now log into the Participant Portal to view their benefit status, track payments, and manage their account.
