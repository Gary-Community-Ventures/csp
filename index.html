Family User Journey

  1. Discovery & Screening

    Services: Universal Screener, Digital Identity

    A family finds support via a universal screener and creates a secure digital ID to save their information, simplifying future applications.

  2. Application & Verification

    Services: Application Service, Digital Identity

    They apply for the CSP, using their digital ID to pre-fill the application. After receiving presumptive eligibility, they are directed to the main app.

  3. Payment & Engagement

    Services: Companion App, Digital Wallet, Provider DB, Content, AI

    The family uses the Companion App to manage payments via their digital wallet, find licensed providers, and receive AI-powered summaries and activity recommendations.
